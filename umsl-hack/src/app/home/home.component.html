What is UMSL|HACK?


UMSL|HACK is an annual hackathon hosted by the Information Systems Department at the University of Missouri-St. Louis. UMSL|HACK main goal is to create a platform to challenge/encourage college students to apply their skills by building an application in 36 hours! We know how valuable hackathons are to college students, while it might be intimidating to have a running application in the allotted time, we work very hard to provide participants with all the mentoring and help they need to get their app running. We hope by participating in UMSL|HACK students will gain valuable skills as they will be working in a team to complete a specific task that requires diverse sets of skills.


UMSL|HACK: Show Me the Data!


UMSL Hack: Show Me the Data! will be held on Friday February 15th from 5:00 PM to Sunday February 17th 6:00 PM at the University of Missouri-St. Louis Express Script Hall. The theme of this event is "Show Me the Data!", where students will work in teams of 3-5 people to create projects focused on creatively by harnessing the power of Data. Our goal is to see how effectively students work with existing or newly created data while creating an interface to solve real problems much as they would encounter in day-to-day business.
