What is UMSL|HACK?


UMSL|HACK is an annual hackathon hosted by the Information Systems Department at the University of Missouri-St. Louis. UMSL|HACK's main goal is to create a platform to challenge/encourage college students to apply their skills by building an application in 36 hours! We know how valuable hackathons are to college students, while it might be intimidating to have a running application in the allotted time, we work very hard to provide participants with all the mentoring and help they need to get their app running. We hope by participating in UMSL|HACK students will gain valuable skills as they will be working in a team to complete a specific task that requires diverse sets of skills.


UMSL|H4CK: HACK THE STACK!


UMSL H4CK: HACK THE STACK! will be held on Friday February 21st from 5:00 PM to Sunday February 23rd 6:00 PM at the University of Missouri-St. Louis Express Script Hall. The theme of this event is "HACK THE STACK", where students will work in teams of 3-5 people to create projects focused on creativity by harnessing the power of Technology Stacks. Our goal is to see how effectively students work with technology to solve real problems much as they would encounter in day-to-day business.
